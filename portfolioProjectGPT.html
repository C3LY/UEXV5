Transcarry

Waterhouse Charters set sail in 2022 to create exclusive, memorable boating experiences for luxury leisure seekers in Grand Cayman. We were tasked with creating a distinct visual identity that would define the brand as the premium option within the industry.

Context
Daring to be different and drawing inspiration from the sleek black and green Axopar that cruises above Cayman's crystal blue waters, the Waterhouse Charters brand was born to reflect the long lines, sexy curves, and overall appeal of the premium vessel.

The premium boat charter experience in the Cayman Islands required a premium brand that stood out from the rest.
